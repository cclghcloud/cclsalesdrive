## Sales Pipeline

### 8 Standard Stages

1. **Lead**
   - New contact captured
   - Initial interest shown
   - Needs qualification

2. **MQL (Marketing Qualified)**
   - Shows buying interest
   - Fits target profile
   - Ready for sales touch

3. **SQL (Sales Qualified)**
   - Actively considering
   - Budget confirmed
   - Decision timeline set

4. **SAL (Sales Accepted)**
   - Sales team accepted
   - High priority deal
   - Active engagement

5. **Opportunity**
   - Active sales process
   - Demo scheduled
   - Proposal phase

6. **Negotiation**
   - Final pricing discussion
   - Terms being finalized
   - Close imminent

7. **Closed Won**
   - Deal successfully closed
   - Contract signed
   - Revenue recognized

8. **Closed Lost**
   - Deal lost to competitor
   - Prospect not ready
   - Archived opportunity
